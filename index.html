    Project Idea

    My project idea consists of using a smartphone, and it’s called Smilenomics. It’s a smartphone app that that lets you send and receive positive messages with strangers when you feel disconnected. By creating this kind of project, I want to be able to connect people and create an atmosphere where people may feel comfortable sharing their thoughts and feelings as well as providing support, and a little pick-me -up for those days that make people feel hopeless. I hope to create a platform where people would be able to share these nice messages and favourite and share them to spread love. We all have those days where being blue is completely all that we feel and it brings out entire day to a halt, stopping it from being the best day it could be. This app would change those days and turn that frown upside down. Studies show that more than 1 in 3 people said they worried (38 percent) or dealt with stress (37 percent), according to the 2018 Global Emotions Report, released on Wednesday by Washington, D.C. (based analytics and consulting firm Gallup). That’s more than a quarter of the world, that experiences unhappiness in their life. This app could change their life, because of the undeniable amount of support users would experience and feel by sharing and receiving positive messages that would put a smile on someone’s face; hence why Smilenomics is a cheap and easily assembled solution to this problem. Features of the app, Smilenomics is a service for the general public, no matter what age. It closely resembles a social media platform, but instead of statuses, there are positive messages that will spread around; with the help of what we call “Supporters” and the “Supported”. If there is an immediate need for a pick-me-up, there will be an option where you tap a button a pop-up would appear, with a delightful message. This message would contain fewer or a large amount of words, anecdotes, a handwritten message and/or voice message. All of these messages will be moderated by the system in case of abusive language or inappropriate content. As a user, you can “heart” publicly posted messages and send friend requests. There will also be report buttons just in case there is someone who is using the app as a platform to spread hate, through hate speech, bullying etc. The tool and technology that I would use is Swift. With this mobile app developer, there is minimal coding involved and the app would be easy to maintain. This would be convenient for me, as I would like to use less coding as possible for the application. I like the designing part of the app, creating sleek and clean designs that would be easier for the user to use. The skills that are needed for this app to be created is to know the different design elements to centre the app around the user, since they will be using this app the most. Skills such as product management, design skills, writing skills, testing skills, coding, cross-platform and data skills will be needed to make this app absolutely successful for a good cause. For the outcome of this app, it would help lots of people from all over the world, people of all ages as the Smilenomics community would have support for each other and be supported through tough times. This would affect people and people’s moods as it would drastically change them for the better. By planting positivity on one person, more and more will spread and grow and it would completely change the world, one smile at a time.
